Neptune

Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun and the fourth largest in our Solar System. It is known for its deep blue color and for being the only planet in the Solar System that is not visible to the naked eye.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 4.5 billion km
  • Length of Day: 16.1 Earth hours
  • Length of Year: 164.8 Earth years
  • Number of Moons: 14
