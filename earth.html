Earth

Earth is the only known planet with an atmosphere containing free oxygen, oceans of liquid water on its surface, and life.

The distance between Earth and the Sun varies by about 3% over the course of a year due to the elliptical shape of Earth's orbit.

The Earth's magnetic field is generated by the movement of molten iron in its core.
