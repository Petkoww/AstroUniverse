Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It is often referred to as the "Blue Planet" due to its abundance of liquid water on its surface.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million km
  • Length of Day: 23.9 hours
  • Length of Year: 365.2 days
  • Number of Moons: 1
