Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is a gas giant. It is named after the Roman king of gods and thunder.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 778.5 million km
  • Length of Day: 9.9 Earth hours
  • Length of Year: 11.9 Earth years
  • Number of Moons: 79
