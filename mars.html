Mars

Mars is a rocky planet and the fourth planet from the sun. It is often called the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 227.9 million km
  • Length of Day: 24.6 Earth hours
  • Length of Year: 687 Earth days
  • Number of Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)
