Mercury

A small, rocky planet in the inner solar system, Mercury is named after the Roman messenger god who traveled quickly between the gods and mortals.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 57.91 million km
  • Length of Day: 58.6 Earth days
  • Length of Year: 87.97 Earth days
  • Number of Moons: 0
