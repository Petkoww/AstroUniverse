Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second largest in our Solar System after Jupiter. It is known for its spectacular ring system, which is composed mostly of ice particles with a smaller amount of rocky debris and dust.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 1.43 billion km
  • Length of Day: 10.7 Earth hours
  • Length of Year: 29.4 Earth years
  • Number of Moons: 82
