Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and the third largest in our Solar System. It is known for its blue-green color and tilted axis, which causes extreme seasons that last for decades.

Basic Information

  • Distance from the Sun: 2.87 billion km
  • Length of Day: 17.2 Earth hours
  • Length of Year: 84.0 Earth years
  • Number of Moons: 27
