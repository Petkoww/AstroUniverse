Venus

Venus is the second planet from the sun and is similar in size and composition to Earth. It is often referred to as Earth's sister planet due to their similarities. However, Venus has a thick atmosphere made primarily of carbon dioxide that creates a runaway greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet in the solar system.

Venus has a retrograde rotation, meaning it rotates in the opposite direction of its orbit around the sun. It also has no moons or rings.
